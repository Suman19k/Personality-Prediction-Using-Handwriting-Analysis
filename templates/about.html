Personality Prediction Based On Handwriting Image

Handwriting reveals the true personality including Anxious, Cooperative, Responsible, Openess and Enthusiastic.

What are we trying to do?

With this project we are trying to find out Personality Prediction and we have different types of class.

Context

Professional handwriting examiners called graphologists often identify the writer with a piece of handwriting.

More about Personality detection from Image

Humans have a variety of emotions and it is difficult to collect enough records for each emotion and hence the problem of class imbalance arises.

Responsible

Characteristics with this class are known for their diligence, thoroughness, and careful attention to detail in their actions. They tend to be goal-oriented individuals who are committed to following through on their commitments and tasks. Traits such as the consistency of handwriting, the neatness of writing, and the presence of attention to detail serve as indicators of a person's level of responsibility.

Co-operative

Characteristics with this class are empathetic, helpful, and trusting, often described as warm and compassionate. Handwriting analysis offers clues to this trait through indicators like the slope, pressure, and fluidity of writing.

Enthusiastic

Charwith this class are typically assertive, talkative, and enthusiastic in social settings, enjoying being around others and often seeking attention. In handwriting analysis, indicators like the size, spacing, slant, and pressure of writing can reveal a person's level of extraversion.

Anxious

Characteristics with this class may be moody, anxious, and easily stressed. In handwriting analysis, traits such as pressure, letter size, spacing, and consistency can indicate anxious levels.

Openess

Characteristics such as handwriting style, decoration, doodles, and overall complexity can indicate a person's level of openness. Those with high openness may showcase varied and creative handwriting, while those with low openness might display more uniform and straightforward writing.
